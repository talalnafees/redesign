FAQ
Who are you?

We are a Punjab, Pakistan-based apparel manufacturer operating since 2019. Our 11–50 person team serves clients across North America (70%), Northern, Western, and Eastern Europe (10% each).

How do you guarantee quality?

Every order gets a pre-production sample for your approval before we start mass production. We also conduct a full final inspection before any shipment leaves our facility.

What's your production capacity?

Our factory runs full production capacity of over 50,000 pieces per month. We support both bulk container shipments and drop shipping services for smaller, ongoing orders.

Do you offer custom branding?

Yes. We have a professional in-house design team that handles logo embroidery, screen printing, heat transfers, woven labels, and custom packaging. Just send us your files.

About the Manufacturer

Impact Apparels is a vertically integrated clothing manufacturer based in Punjab, Pakistan. Since 2019, we have expanded our trade and commercial intercourse throughout the world, with a core focus on performance-driven sportswear for international brands and retailers.
